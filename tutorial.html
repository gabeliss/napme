NapMe

Tutorial

Start by select the type of nap you would like.

A Power Nap will get you rejuvinated in 15 min, while a Normal or Recovery Nap will take 30 and 60 min, respectively.

You can change alarm sounds and select a snooze option in the settings page below.

If you change your mind after setting a timer, you can cancel at anytime by clicking the "Cancel" button

Help us make the app work better for you by providing feedback after your refreshing nap!
